Rails is awesome, but what makes it great is Ruby. I found myself learning Rails without knowing Ruby. Don't make my mistake. Some books:

"The Ruby Programming Language" from O'Reilly
"The Ruby Way" and "Design Patterns in Ruby" from Addison-Wesley
"Ruby for Rails" from Manning
